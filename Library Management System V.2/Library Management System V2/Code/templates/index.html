In a world of words, a comedy unfolds,
Where tales are told in laughter's hold.
Characters dance on pages so bright,
In the carnival of books, a pure delight.

With every turn, a joke takes flight,
Tickling minds in the literary night.
In this funny language of ink and prose,
Reading becomes the best joke we compose.

In the library of life, each book a jest,
Where humor and wisdom coexist best.
Through pages, we giggle, laughter's embrace,
In the funky language, books leave their trace.
